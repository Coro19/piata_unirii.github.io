UNION SQUARE

One of Timisoara's main squares

A place of historical signifficance for our city, originally named Losonczy Square, after Stefan Losonczy, killed by the turks in 1552, when they invaded the stronghold of Timisoara. It is also known as the Dome Square, due to the Catholic Dome. The name of Union Square was given in 1919, because this is the place where the romanian troops stopped when they entered Timisoara.
